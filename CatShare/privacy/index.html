CatShare 隐私权政策

生效日期：2025年4月11日

CatShare 是一款通过 Wi-Fi 直连或本地网络进行文件和文本分享的应用程序。本应用非常重视您的隐私权，并致力于保护您的个人数据。

1. 我们收集哪些数据？

  • 文件名称和大小：用于显示分享内容。
  • 剪贴板文本内容：在用户明确同意接收时，临时写入本地剪贴板。
  • 缩略图（如有）：仅在本地设备缓存，用于展示。

这些数据不会被传输至任何远程服务器。

2. 数据存储与处理方式

所有数据均在设备本地处理。CatShare 不包含任何云同步或远程服务器通信。

3. 第三方服务

CatShare 不集成任何第三方广告或分析 SDK。

4. 面向儿童

CatShare 并未专为 13 岁以下儿童设计，也不会特意收集儿童的任何个人信息。

5. 用户控制与选择

  • 用户可随时取消传输请求。
  • 接收前需用户明确操作确认。
  • 剪贴板内容不会自动读取，仅在明确接收文本内容时使用。

6. 联系我们

CatShare Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 11, 2025

CatShare is an application for sharing files and text over Wi-Fi Direct or a local network. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. What data do we collect?

  • File names and sizes: used to display the transfer details.
  • Clipboard text content: only stored locally when the user explicitly agrees to receive it.
  • Thumbnails (if any): temporarily cached on your device for display purposes.

This data is never uploaded or sent to any remote server.

2. Data storage and processing

All data is processed locally on your device. CatShare does not include any cloud synchronization or remote communication features.

3. Third-party services

CatShare does not integrate any third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

4. Children

CatShare is not intended for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from them.

5. User control and consent

  • Users can cancel transfer requests at any time.
  • Transfers require explicit user confirmation before proceeding.
  • Clipboard content is never accessed automatically.
